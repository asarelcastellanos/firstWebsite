One thing I like to do in my spare time is to play video games. When I get home from school I usually do my homework then play video games. This ranges from 2k and FIFA to games on my laptop like CSGO, GTA or Indie games. I usually play about 3 hours if I can, and I play with my friends from school.

Another thing I like to do in my spare time is to play soccer with my friends or practice soccer. I have been playing soccer all my life either with a team or just by myself. I also watch it when I can. I usually try to play with friends over the weekend, I try to practice during the week. I practice with some players of our school soccer team or on my own.

Another thing I like to do in my spare time is to play the drums. I started to play the drums around middle school and haven't stopped since. I have been self taught a bit and had someone teach me. I started to play because some of my family are musicians and they encouraged me. I really enjoy playing the drums and over the years of learning I have become more confident in my ability to play and I have even picked up the Bass Guitar and started self teaching myself that. I usually practice that over the weekend because my week can get so busy.
