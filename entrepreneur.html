Noah Glass

Noah Glass is an American software developer, best known for Twitter. AFter leaving Industrial Light and Magic, Glass world on a couple of projects with Marc Canter, founder of Marcromind which would become Macromedia, which would later make Flash animation and multimedia software. In 2006 Glass helped develop and come up with the idea that would soon become Twitter. He was also acknowledge as coining the name "Twitter".
